The Online Privacy Policy of the iSenhas website (https://isenhas.com.br) was created to comply with the LGPD (General Data Protection Law) and to affirm our commitment to the security and privacy of the information collected from visitors to our site. This policy is subject to occasional updates, and we recommend that it be consulted periodically. You can visit our site to learn about our services and products, check out our offers, read articles, obtain information, and support without having to provide any personal information. However, if this occurs, this policy seeks to clarify how we collect and handle your personal data.



How We Handle Data

  1. Any information provided by users will be collected and stored according to current security and reliability standards.
  2. We use the standard HTTPS protocol on our site to ensure that all information collected from users is transmitted securely, utilizing standard Internet encryption processes.
  3. The personal information provided to us will be collected through legal means. This collection will be for the purpose of commercial communication so that we can sell our services and products, and provide support and service regarding them.
  4. Unless we have legal or judicial determination, user information will never be provided to third parties or used for purposes other than those for which it was collected.
  5. Access to the collected information is restricted to the managers of iSenhas, and we will maintain the integrity of the information provided to us.
  6. We may occasionally use cookies (*) to verify your identity, personalize your access, and monitor the use of our website to improve its navigation and functionality.
  7. We provide customer service channels for our users to clarify any doubts that may arise regarding their data. These channels can be accessed through the email davi@isenhas.com.br.



How We Collect Data



Consent or Blocking Cookies

For the data collection features on our site to work properly, the use of cookies (small text files that can set preferences) is necessary. Cookies are not programs, do not steal data from your computer, and do not jeopardize your browsing, but you have the right to block our cookies if you wish.



Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • The right to block any type of tracker and cookie generated on our site so that no information about your connection and access to our site is shared.
  • The right to ask us to update any incorrect personal information we have about you.
  • The right to opt-out of any commercial and marketing communications.
  • The right to request the removal of any personal data we have stored about you.

We reserve a period of 5 business days to respond to any contact regarding your personal data, which may be extended during holiday periods, extended holidays, recess, and collective vacations.
